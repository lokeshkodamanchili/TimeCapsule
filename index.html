About the Project

TimeCapsule ‚Äì Unlock the Past, Ace the Future is an academic knowledge-sharing platform developed to preserve valuable study materials from seniors and make them accessible to juniors.

The system allows seniors to upload notes, PDFs, assignments, and important resources, which are securely stored and organized using Microsoft Excel, SharePoint, and Power Automate.
